Who We Are

Grow and protect your wealth

Since 2016 · Nairobi

Investcent Investment Bank

Investcent is a Nairobi-based boutique investment bank, founded in 2016 as Investcent Partners to meet the needs of those seeking to grow and protect their wealth, and licensed as an investment bank since 2021. We manage retail, institutional and pension capital with one focus: generating alpha through disciplined, hands-on active management.

Our team consists of highly skilled professionals and successful investors with deep experience trading financial markets, operating with the latest technology and investing software. We are active across every liquid asset class: equities, fixed income, FX, derivatives and alternatives, as an NSE Authorised Securities Dealer with real-time execution.

The growth in the local capital market over the past decade, driven in part by the government's need to finance development, has created a larger, more liquid and tradeable market. As participants have become more advanced, so have the products we use to capture these opportunities for our clients.

RBA-Licensed Retirement Solutions

Pension Funds

We manage pension capital under RBA regulation with one goal: superior, risk-managed returns for schemes and their members. We offer both individual and umbrella pension schemes, and trustees choose between two investment mandate structures, all custom-built to the fund's investment objectives with full reporting and oversight.

Our Pension Schemes
INDIVIDUAL

Individual Pension Scheme

A personal retirement plan for employed, self-employed and informal-sector savers. You contribute at your own pace, your savings remain fully portable when you change jobs, and contributions attract applicable tax benefits, all under RBA-regulated trust protection.

UMBRELLA

Umbrella Pension Scheme

Multiple employers participate under one professionally governed trust, sharing administration, custody and management costs. Ideal for SMEs and groups that want a full occupational scheme for their staff without the expense of running one alone.

Our Investment Mandate Structures
RBA-LICENSED

Special Mandate

We manage a defined asset class or investment strategy within a pension scheme's portfolio; alpha-seeking active management strategies that complement your existing managers.

RBA-LICENSED

Full Mandate

End-to-end management of the pension fund's assets under RBA regulation, with full reporting, prudent oversight and mandates custom-built to each fund's investment objectives.

Our difference: we actively trade fixed income.

Most managers buy bonds and hold them to maturity, accepting whatever the market pays. As an NSE Authorised Securities Dealer with real-time execution, we actively trade the yield curve: capturing capital gains from repricing, rotating across tenors, and locking in opportunities the buy-and-hold market leaves on the table. That is how our pension portfolios target returns above the benchmark, not at it.

An SMA is a dedicated portfolio managed exclusively for you, with direct ownership of every security rather than units in a pooled fund. You see exactly what you hold, why you hold it, and what it costs, while our team applies the same active investment discipline used across the firm.

FAQs

Questions investors ask us.

Everything you need to know about opening an account, investing, fees and withdrawals.

How do I open an account?
You complete an application (online or physical), provide identification and KYC documents (ID/passport, KRA PIN and a photo), select your fund(s) and currency, and transfer funds to the designated client account. You can start the Money Market Fund with just KES 2,000 (or USD 20).
Can I invest via mobile money or online banking?
Yes, through our official payment channels: bank transfer to our SBM Bank collection accounts (shown at the bottom of this page) or via M-Pesa Paybill. Always quote your investment account number as the reference.
How do I top up my investment?
Add to your existing account using the same payment channels, referencing your investment account number (format HKXXXX for KES, HUXXXX for USD). Share the payment confirmation with us for timely allocation.
How do I track performance and statements?
You receive monthly statements and can view up-to-date prices and yields on our website and digital platforms, or through your member portal. Further detail is published in our fact sheets and newsletters on an ongoing basis. A mobile app is in development.
Can I switch from one fund to another?
Yes, simply fill in the switch form provided on our website.
How can I obtain more information on fund performance?
More information is available on our website and in our fact sheets and newsletters, which are published on an ongoing basis. See the Research section.
Can I open an account for a minor or joint investors?
Yes. Accounts for minors (operated by a parent/guardian) and joint accounts are available, subject to completion of the relevant application forms and KYC.
What is the difference between the KES and USD funds?
KES funds are denominated in Kenya shillings and target local-currency assets. USD funds are denominated in US dollars and primarily invest in dollar assets or USD-linked instruments, providing currency diversification.
Are these funds suitable for both individuals and institutions?
Yes. Both retail and institutional investors can invest, subject to minimum investment amounts and KYC requirements.
What sets Investcent apart?
Active trading. Most managers buy and hold; as an NSE Authorised Securities Dealer we actively trade every liquid asset class, especially fixed income, to capture returns above the benchmark, with disciplined risk limits.
Who regulates these funds?
All our funds (KES and USD) are regulated by the Capital Markets Authority (CMA) under the Capital Markets Act and the Collective Investment Schemes Regulations. Pension mandates are regulated by the Retirement Benefits Authority (RBA).
How do you manage risk in the various funds?
Through diversification across asset classes and issuers, active asset allocation, ongoing monitoring, and strict adherence to regulatory and internal risk limits. Note that returns are not guaranteed and past performance is not an indication of future performance.
Can I schedule a consultation to discuss my financial needs?
Yes, reach us on +254 709 327 800 or info@investcent.co, or use the contact form on the Get in Touch page, and a member of the team will set up a consultation.
